about

Swatchhub was founded by Brianna Fano in 2016. After working with Fashion Industry leaders like Tory Burch and J. Mendel she started SwatchHub. SwatchHub kicked off with a small collection of Swatches for purchase. After some initial success Brianna grew the collection by including Embroidery Designerâ€™s concepts from around the world to the collection.

Initially SwatchHub was an afterwork project, she spent a lot of her free time making and selling in ways to start this business after her full-time work. In 2018 she was running SwatchHub full time and began taking Custom Embroidery projects using her Embroidery Machine and Vinyl cutter.

Now, in 2020 we have grown into a studio space in Industry City working with Designers on Innovation and execution of their Embellishment concept. In the last year we have partnered with factories in India to execute full product sampling and production

Our BK studio now has in-house designers and machinery that is capable of executing a wide variety of textiles, and patterns. We have most recently launched a project called SwatchBuddy where we embroidery customized drawings into SwatchBuddy dolls.

We are so excited to connect and continue to grow this community!
